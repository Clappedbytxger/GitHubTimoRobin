Studio kamiOur studio

Desa Mas — desa para pengukirMas — the village of carvers

Jika Ubud dikenal sebagai desa pelukis, Desa Mas adalah desa para pengukir kayu. Di sanggar keluarga Pande, kami mengukir topeng, patung dewa, dan relief dari kayu pilihan — dengan sabar, detail demi detail.

Keahlian ini diwariskan turun-temurun. Setiap karya membawa jiwa dan cerita Bali.

If Ubud is the village of painters, Mas is the village of wood carvers. In the Pande family compound we carve masks, statues of gods and reliefs from selected wood — patiently, detail by detail.

The skill is passed down through generations. Each piece carries the soul and stories of Bali.
