Rishton — the ceramic heart of Central AsiaRishton — Markaziy Osiyoning sopol yuragi

For centuries, Rishton has been the centre of Central Asian ceramics. Using local red clay and the secret ishkor glaze — made from the ash of mountain plants — we create the unmistakable turquoise and cobalt of the Silk Road.

Each plate, bowl and lagan is thrown and hand-painted with flowing floral and geometric patterns. No two are the same.

Asrlar davomida Rishton Markaziy Osiyo sopolchiligining markazi bo'lib kelgan. Mahalliy qizil loy va sirli ishqor siri — tog' o'simliklari kulidan tayyorlangan — yordamida biz Ipak yo'lining betakror feruza va ko'k ranglarini yaratamiz.

Har bir lagancha, kosa va lagan qo'lda bo'yaladi. Ikkitasi bir xil emas.
